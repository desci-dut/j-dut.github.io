BIOGRAPHY:

Zhen Zhang received his Ph. D. in management science and engineering from Dalian university of Technology in 2014, supervised by Prof. Chonghui Guo. He also holds a B.S. in Engineering Management from China University of Petroleum (East China). From Oct. 2012 to Nov. 2013, he was a visiting Ph. D. student with the Decision Systems and e-Service Intelligence Laboratory, Centre for Quantum Computation & Intelligent Systems, University of Technology, Sydney under the supervision of Prof. Jie Lu and Guangquan Zhang. He is now an assistant professor with the Institute of Systems Engineering, Faculty of Management and Economics, Dalian University of Technology. His current research interests include (fuzzy) group decision making, multi-criteria decision making, preference modelling, bi-level decision making, clustering algorithms with applications. His work has been published on refereed journals and conference proceedings, including Knowledge-Based Systems (KBS), Computers & Industrial Engineering, International Journal of Computational Intelligence Systems (IJCIS), International Journal of Systems Science (IJSS), Physica A and International Journal of Knowledge and Systems Science, among others. Since 2008, He has been the principal participant of several research projects granted by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and National High Technology Research and Development Program. He has been a peer reviewer of more than 14 international journals and 5 international conferences, such as European Journal of Operational Research, Group Decision and Negotiation, IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, IJCIS, IJSS, International Journal of Production Research, KBS, Technological and Economic Development of Econom and IFSA-NAFIPS conference. He was the receiver of 2012 "Doctoral Student Academic Star" of Dalian University of Technology and was awarded the excellent graduate of Liaoning Province in 2014.
