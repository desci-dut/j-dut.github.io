BIOGRAPHY:

Zhen Zhang received his B.S. in Engineering Management from China University of Petroleum (Eastern China) in Jul. 2008. He was a postgraduate of the Institute of Systems Engineering, Dalian University of Technology, China from Sep. 2008 to Mar. 2010, and he is now working towards his Ph. D. in management science and engineering with the same university. His current research interests include group decision making, data mining and business intelligence. He has several papers published on refereed journals and conference proceedings, including Knowledge-Based Systems (KBS), International Journal of Knowledge and Systems Science (IJKSS), Journal of the China Society for Scientific and Technical Information (JCSSTI), MCS and KSS, among others. Since 2008, He has been the principal participant of serval research projects granted by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and National High Technology Research and Development Program. He was the recipient of the "Shengli Chengcai" Award of China University of Petroleum in 2005, and he was also reviewed as an excellent graduate of Shandong Province in 2008. In Oct. 2011, he was rewarded as an excellent doctoral student of Dalian University of Technology.
