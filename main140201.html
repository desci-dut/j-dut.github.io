BIOGRAPHY:

Zhen Zhang received his B.S. in Engineering Management from China University of Petroleum (East China) in July 2008. He is now working towards his Ph. D. in management science and engineering with the Institute of Systems Engineering, Dalian university of Technology. From Oct. 2012 to Nov. 2013, he was a visiting Ph. D. student with the Decision Systems and e-Service Intelligence Laboratory, Centre for Quantum Computation & Intelligent Systems, University of Technology, Sydney. His current research interests include (fuzzy) group decision making, multi-criteria decision making, multi-level decision making, data mining and business intelligence. His work has been published on refereed journals and conference proceedings, including Knowledge-Based Systems (KBS), International Journal of Computational Intelligence Systems (IJCIS), International Journal of Knowledge and Systems Science (IJKSS), Control and Decision, MCS and EUSFLAT, among others. Since 2008, He has been the principal participant of several research projects granted by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and National High Technology Research and Development Program. He has been the reviewer of several international journals and conferences, such as European Journal of Operational Research (EJOR), IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, KBS, Group Decision and Negotiation (GDN), IJCIS, International Journal of Systems Science, Technological and Economic Development of Economy (TEDE) , African Journal of Business Management, MCS workshop, ISKE conference, KSEM conference, and IFSA-NAFIPS conference. In Nov. 2012, he was rewarded as the "Doctoral Student Academic Star" of Dalian University of Technology. For any enquires, just contact me via zhangzhen@mail.dlut.edu.cn.
