BIOGRAPHY:

Zhen Zhang received his Bachelor degree of Management in Engineering Management from China University of Petroleum (Eastern China) in Jul. 2008, and he was recommended to Dalian University of Technology as a post graduate student without taking the entrance examinations in 2007. He was a master degree candidate of the Institute of Systems Engineering, Dalian University of Technology, China from Sep. 2008 to Mar. 2010, and he is now studying as a Ph. D. candidate, majoring in management science and engineering in Dalian University of Technology. His current research interests are data mining techniques and decision analysis methods. He was the recipient of the “Shengli Chengcai” Award of China University of Petroleum in 2005, and he was also been reviewed as an excellent graduate of Shandong Province in 2008. Recently, he was rewarded as an excellent graduate student of Dalian University of Technology.
